About Horgan Brothers, Inc.

Horgan Brothers Incorporated provides full-service site improvements, including demo, erosion control, excavation, utilities, concrete, & paving. We pride ourselves on having highly trained, skilled long term employees who are extremely efficient in the field and knowledgeable in all aspects of construction. With over 40 years experience, Horgan Brothers Inc. assures quality on all its jobs by assigning a project manager as well as a field supervisor who make certain projects stay on schedule and within budget. In addition we provide valuable engineering consulting to the owner on all projects and have a strong reputation for completing fast track and on time while maintaining strict quality control.

Site Work

Site Work Services include:

  • Excavation
  • Utilities
  • Residential Concrete
  • Commercial Concrete
  • Paving

Horgan Brothers Inc. Excavating is a premier excavation and site development contractor in Eastern PA. Horgan Brothers Inc. has been in the earthwork business for over 40 years & has completed hundreds of major earthwork projects. Our commitment to our clients is to maintain the highest level of quality while delivering cost efficient results and meeting the most demanding schedules. Over 100 pieces of equipment combined with experienced operators results in greater project efficiency. In conjunction with our use of the AGTEK system Horgan Brothers Inc. has a highly efficient tool for modeling, analyzing, digitizing, & documenting dirt; resulting in better grade management and lower costs for customers. Our fleet of tri-axles & heavy equipment gives us the capabilities of moving earth regardless of the job. We also specialize in highly complicated projects.

Recycling

Horgan Recycling Inc. accepts concrete, blacktop, brick, block and rock materials and recycles these materials into 2A modified rap stone for road, parking lot & driveway sub base . In addition our finished product provides excellent compaction qualities for backfill of utility trenches where full depth stone backfill is required.
