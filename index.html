About Horgan Brothers, Inc.

Horgan Brothers Incorporated provides full-service site improvements, including demo, erosion control, excavation, utilities, concrete, & paving. We pride ourselves on having highly trained, skilled long term employees who are extremely efficient in the field and knowledgeable in all aspects of construction. With over 40 years experience, Horgan Brothers Inc. assures quality on all its jobs by assigning a project manager as well as a field supervisor who make certain projects stay on schedule and within budget. In addition we provide valuable engineering consulting to the owner on all projects and have a strong reputation for completing fast track and on time while maintaining strict quality control.
